# Research

Research industry standards and best practices before designing a feature.

## When to use
Before designing significant features. Invoke with `/research [topic]`.

## Instructions

### 1. Frame the question
- What decision are we making?
- What are our constraints?

### 2. Research
- How do established products do it?
- What's the standard/protocol?
- What do users expect?
- What's the 80/20 — simplest solution covering most cases?

### 3. Evaluate for our context
- Does it fit our tech stack?
- Does it fit our user base?
- What's the maintenance cost?

## Output format
```
## Research: [topic]

### How others do it
- [Product]: [approach]

### Options
| | Option A | Option B |
|---|---|---|

### Recommendation
[Pick with reasoning]

### Skills to update
[Which execution skills need research findings translated into them]
```
